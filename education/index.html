Use the Fertility Window Calculator

Fertility timing refers to estimating ovulation and the days when conception is more likely based on menstrual cycle patterns. These estimates rely on cycle length and timing assumptions rather than direct biological measurement. Timing can vary from cycle to cycle due to natural variability.

These explanations are based on general cycle timing patterns and may not reflect individual biological variation in every case.
