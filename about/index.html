About QuietDue

QuietDue is a privacy-first reproductive timing and fertility awareness utility. It provides structured estimates of ovulation timing and fertile windows based on user-entered cycle information.

The purpose of QuietDue is to offer a simple, informational timing reference without requiring account creation, storing cycle history, or collecting personal reproductive data.

All calculations occur locally within your browser. QuietDue does not transmit or store personal timing inputs.

QuietDue does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. It is an educational timing tool designed to explain how fertility window estimates are calculated.
