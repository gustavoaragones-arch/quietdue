About QuietDue

Pregnancy can bring more questions than answers—especially in the earliest days. QuietDue was created for those moments of uncertainty, offering a private and reassuring space to find clarity without judgment or exposure.

We believe information about your body should belong only to you. That’s why QuietDue never collects personal details or creates tracking profiles. Every calculation happens quietly in your browser, leaving no digital footprint behind.

Our approach is simple: gentle design, accurate guidance, and complete discretion. QuietDue isn’t a medical service—it’s a quiet tool built with empathy for anyone navigating an early or uncertain pregnancy.

Here, privacy is protection, and calm is our default. We’re here so you can take a breath, find your answers, and move forward—quietly, in your own time.

What it does

You enter the first day of your last menstrual period. QuietDue estimates a due date, current gestational age, and a confidence window. It also shows early milestones as approximate windows—such as when many people first confirm pregnancy, and when a first ultrasound is often offered. These are ranges, not exact dates. It’s normal if things feel unclear. All of this runs entirely in your browser. Nothing is saved, tracked, or sent anywhere.
