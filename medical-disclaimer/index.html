Medical Disclaimer

Please read this before using QuietDue.

Informational only

QuietDue provides estimates for informational purposes only. It is not medical advice, a diagnosis, or a recommendation for treatment. It does not replace advice from a healthcare provider.

Limitations of estimates

Early pregnancy dating is imprecise. Due dates are typically estimated from the first day of the last menstrual period, but cycle length and ovulation timing vary from person to person. Ultrasound may provide different dates than LMP-based calculations.

QuietDue shows a confidence window (±12 days) to reflect this uncertainty. Estimates may change as more information becomes available.

Consult a healthcare provider

For questions about your pregnancy, health, or care options, please consult a healthcare provider. QuietDue cannot and does not provide personalized medical advice.

No doctor-patient relationship

Using QuietDue does not create a doctor-patient or clinician-patient relationship. We do not provide care, diagnose conditions, or make treatment recommendations.

Your choices

QuietDue does not direct you toward any particular outcome. Whatever your situation, your choices are yours. This tool is here to offer clarity when you want it—nothing more.
