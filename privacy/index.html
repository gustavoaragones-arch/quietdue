Privacy

QuietDue is built so that your information stays with you. This page explains how that works.

How QuietDue works

QuietDue runs entirely in your browser. When you enter a date and receive an estimate, all of that happens on your device. No dates, no results, and no other information are sent to our servers or anyone else’s.

What we don’t do

  • No accounts. You don’t need to sign up or log in.
  • No tracking. We don’t use analytics, pixels, or any form of tracking.
  • No cookies. We don’t set or read cookies.
  • No data storage. We don’t store your dates or results in a database. Nothing is saved server-side.

What happens to your data

When you use the calculator, your date and results exist only in your browser’s memory. If you refresh the page or close the tab, that information is gone. We never see it.

If you use “Add to Calendar,” an .ics file is generated on your device and downloaded. We don’t receive or store that file.

If you print, the print happens locally. We don’t receive or store the printed content.

Hosting

QuietDue is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. When you load the site, Cloudflare serves the static HTML, CSS, and JavaScript files. That is a standard web request; we do not collect or process personal information from it.

Your choices

You control what you do with your results. You can close the tab, print, or add dates to your calendar. We don’t need to know.

Use the calculator

QuietDue does not store or transmit your data.

This tool provides estimates for informational purposes only. It is not medical advice.
