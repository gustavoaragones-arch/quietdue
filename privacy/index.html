Privacy Policy

Data Collection

QuietDue does not require account creation and does not store menstrual cycle dates, ovulation estimates, or fertility window inputs. All timing calculations are performed locally in your browser.

Personal Information

QuietDue does not request names, health records, or identifying personal information.

Cookies

QuietDue does not use tracking cookies related to reproductive timing data. Basic technical cookies may be used by the hosting provider for site functionality.

Hosting

QuietDue is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Standard server logs may be processed by the hosting provider for security and performance purposes.

Data Sharing

QuietDue does not sell, rent, or share personal reproductive timing information.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact hello@quietdue.com.
